Use the Group Dashboard to run your group day to day. This is where organizers manage events, team coordination, member communication, and sponsors.

Path: `/dashboard/group`

<Note>
  If you are still selecting the right workspace, read [Choose Your Dashboard](/getting-started/choose-dashboard).
</Note>

## Access and context

To operate here, you need:

1. A logged-in session.
2. Group-team membership.
3. A selected community and group.

If the right community or group is not selected yet, some actions stay unavailable until you pick them.

## Roles and permissions

Group role permissions are fixed and enforced by middleware plus database checks:

| Group role | Group read | Events | Members | Settings | Sponsors | Team |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| `admin` | Yes | Write | Write | Write | Write | Write |
| `events-manager` | Yes | Write | Read only | Read only | Read only | Read only |
| `viewer` | Yes | Read only | Read only | Read only | Read only | Read only |

### Community role interaction

- Community `admin` and `groups-manager` also have group write permissions inside that community.
- Community `viewer` remains read-only at group scope.

<Info>
  Controls are disabled when your role does not allow an action. Server-side authorization still applies regardless of UI state.
</Info>

## Settings: group identity

Use **Settings** to maintain the information people rely on before joining or attending.

You can manage:

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Identity fields">
    Name, category, and short and long descriptions.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Branding assets">
    Logo, banner, and mobile banner images. If any are not set, OCG falls back to the community equivalents.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Location">
    Location search and map coordinates for in-person groups.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Social links">
    Links to the group's external profiles and website.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Optional content">
    Tags, photo gallery, and extra links.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

Category and region options in this form come from the community's **Group Categories** and **Regions** tabs in the Community Dashboard.

**Brand inheritance model:**

- If a group logo is not set, OCG falls back to the community logo.
- If a group banner or mobile banner is not set, OCG falls back to the community banner.

Field requirements and limits are shown inline in the settings form while editing.

## Team: organizer capacity

**Team** supports invitation-driven organizer management with role updates for existing members.

### Assignable roles

- `admin`
- `events-manager`
- `viewer`

<Warning>
  The last accepted group admin cannot be removed or demoted. Add another accepted team member first, then retry.
</Warning>

This protection ensures continuity for critical event operations and approvals.

    When you add a group team member, OCG sends an invitation with a link to **User Dashboard → Invitations**. For invitation acceptance and dashboard visibility details, see the [User Dashboard Guide](/guides/user-dashboard).

## Analytics: delivery health

Group analytics focuses on operational output:

- Members
- Events
- Attendees
- Page views for the group page and all event pages

Each metric includes running totals and monthly trends, making it easier to tell whether growth is steady over time or mainly tied to isolated spikes. The **Page views** section also includes a daily chart for the last month for each page type.

<Info>
  Analytics values can lag briefly due to caching.
</Info>

## Members: communication

**Members** provides two practical capabilities:

1. Browse member list and join dates.
2. Send plain-text email to all group members.

**Send email** reaches both group members and group team members.

## Sponsors: reusable profiles

Sponsors are managed once and reused across events, reducing repetitive event setup.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create sponsor records">
    Add sponsor profiles in the **Sponsors** tab. Include name, logo, and any relevant links.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Attach sponsors to events">
    When editing an event, attach sponsors from your reusable records in the **Hosts & Speakers** section.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Update once, reflect everywhere">
    Update sponsor details in one place to keep all future events consistent.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Events: operations hub

Most organizer time is spent in the **Events** tab: creating drafts, publishing, managing CFS, reviewing submissions, and running attendance and check-in flows.

### List classification

| List | Criteria |
|---|---|
| Upcoming events | Start time has not yet passed |
| Past events | Start time has already passed |

### Starting an event

**Add Event** opens a structured editor with tabbed sections that map directly to delivery needs: details, schedule, roles, sessions, CFS, and attendees.

For complete event mechanics — draft creation, publishing, CFS workflow, Zoom integration, and event-day checklist — continue to the [Event Operations Guide](/guides/event-operations).

To understand how attendees experience the published result, see the [Public Site Guide](/guides/public-site).
